# Ideal Seller Profile

Welcome back. In this lesson, we're diving into our ideal seller profile, or ISP. Now that we know more about our sellers, let's review what we have learned and how we will respond to ensure we provide the best support possible. First, let's review who our seller is. Our target is a residential property owner, typically a distressed seller looking for an alternative to the traditional real estate market that often feels overwhelming. We've learned these sellers can be individual homeowners, small independent landlords managing a few properties, or even executors of an estate who have inherited a home they need to handle quickly and efficiently.

We focus on individual homeowners or small landlords with up to 10 properties. We look all throughout the Midwest, specifically in Kansas City, St. Louis, Dayton, and Lake of the Ozarks. These are the regions where our expertise can make the most difference. These owners need speed, certainty, and convenience. Often, their properties are difficult to sell through traditional real estate channels, and they need a faster, more reliable solution than a standard listing. What are they feeling? Often, the property is damaged, neglected, or requires significant repairs they simply cannot afford to make right now, causing them immense stress and uncertainty.

Financial pressure is a major factor. This includes unpaid taxes, liens, mortgage issues, or even the looming risk of a foreclosure process that could potentially upend their lives. Life events like divorce, inheritance, or sudden relocation can force a fast decision. In these moments, our quick close model provides the perfect solution for their immediate needs. Triggers are key for identification. An inherited property that a seller doesn't want to manage or renovate is a primary reason they reach out to us for help. Facing foreclosure, bankruptcy, or a tax sale creates an urgent need for the speed and certainty that the BMH Group provides every single day for our clients.

Properties with severe damage from fire, floods, or long-term neglect are prime candidates for our as-is purchasing model. We also help landlords who are ready to exit the market and stop managing tenants, looking for a clean and simple way out of their property burdens. How do we qualify them? Our hard filters are strict to ensure success. The seller must want to close within 30 days and must be the legal owner or have authority. They should not be currently working with a realtor and must be willing to trade a portion of their equity for the incredible speed and convenience we offer at the BMH Group.

There are people we must avoid to protect our time and theirs. Disqualifiers include properties already listed with a realtor or sellers who simply lack the legal authority to sell. Listen for key phrases like, I just want to sell the house as is, I don't want to deal with realtors, or I need to sell fast. These are our signals for success. Our messaging must resonate with their needs. Focus on the speed of closing and the certainty of a cash offer without any of the traditional delays or complications. Always emphasize that we require no repairs, no commissions, and no cleanup. This simple, empathetic process is what truly resonates with our distressed property sellers.

During discovery, start by asking about the property's condition and uncovering their true motivation. We need to see exactly what is driving the potential sale to help them best. Ask how soon they hope to close and if there are specific repairs or legal issues. This helps ensure they meet our strict timeline and condition filters for a fast close. Check the occupancy status and verify if you are speaking with the legal owner. This information is vital for the next steps in our business process to ensure a smooth transition. Ask if they have considered a realtor or if they are specifically looking for a faster option. We want to be their simple, effective, and trustworthy solution.

Finally, ask the powerful closing question. If we could make the process simple and close quickly, would that solve the situation for you? More on this later when we talk about objection handling. Mastering this profile and our response strategy will help you focus your efforts. Let's get to work and help our sellers move forward toward a brighter future.
